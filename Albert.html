DONUT CHART - Total Sales Per Retailer

Chart Overview

This interactive chart offers a comprehensive view of Adidas sales data, showcasing total sales per retailer and total sales versus product categories such as Apparel, Street Footwear, and Athletic Footwear. Users can dynamically filter the data by gender (male, female) and year (2020, 2021) using a slicer, enabling them to explore different visualizations based on their selection. This interactivity enhances user engagement and facilitates deeper analysis of sales trends and patterns over time and across gender demographics.
