ANIMATED CLUSTERED BAR CHART - Operating Profit Over Top 10 States

Chart Overview

The animated clustered bar chart provides a dynamic overview of operating profit and sales method trends across various retailers and time periods. Each cluster represents a retailer, with bars indicating different years and states. Animation over time reveals trends, seasonal patterns, and fluctuations, offering insights into performance disparities, trends over time, effectiveness of sales methods, and regional differences. This visualization aids in understanding and optimizing Adidas's US sales strategy by identifying opportunities for improvement and growth
