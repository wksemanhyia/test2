RADIAL STACKED BAR CHART-UNIT SOLD PER STATE

Chart Overview

The radial stacked bar chart was used to visualize the total units sold for each of the United States. It was an effective visualization tool for representing the hierarchical data with multiple categories. In this case, it displays the sales of different product categories (apparel, athletic footwear, and street footwear) across various states. The radial layout optimizes space utilization and facilitates pattern recognition, making it easier to identify states with higher overall sales or product categories that contribute significantly to total sales. The stacked bars within each state segment allow for clear comparison across product categories, while the circular arrangement aids in visualizing proportions and distributions.
