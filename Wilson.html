RADAR CHART - TOTAL OPERATING PROFIT PER PRODUCT

Chart Overview

This visualization of US Adidas sales data consists of a radar chart and a popup bar chart to display total operating profit per product. The radar chart represents the total operating profit per product. Each point on the radar chart represents a product, and hovering over a point shows the total profit for that product. The popup Chart shows a bar chart for a selected product, year and retailer. It appears when a point on the radar chart is clicked. The user Interaction allows users to select a specific year and retailer from dropdown menus to filter the data and update the charts accordingly.
